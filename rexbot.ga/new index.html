About

Rex is a very easy-to-use and multipurpose Discord bot for your Discord server! It has over 100 commands, which are divided into 10 categories. Rex is highly configurable and includes many useful functions, such as automoderation against spam, swearing, links and raids, moderation commands, welcome announcements with autorole, a ticket system using reactions, memes and cute images of animals, a level system, a customizable prefix, server statistics, funny games and much more!
What are you waiting for? Invite Rex to your own Discord server!

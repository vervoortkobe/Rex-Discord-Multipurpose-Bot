  • automod
    ⚙️ Configuration
    Enables/Disables modules of the automod
    - Antiswear: Deletes messages if they contain a swear word
    - Antispam: Deletes messages if they are spam (Checks every channel, except channels that have "spam" in their name)
    - Antiinvites: Deletes messages if they contain a Discord invite
    - Antiraid: Bans members if their account isn't older than 3 days
    r.automod <antiswear/antispam/antiinvites/antiraid/all> <enable/disable>
